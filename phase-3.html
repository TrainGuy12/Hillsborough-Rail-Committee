USF & Grey Line

USF Line + Grey Line. USF Line 12.5 mi · 14 stops · Grey Line 1.6 mi · 1 stop. Projected to carry 9.6M / year (Yr 5).

USF Line 12.5 mi · 14 stops · Grey Line 1.6 mi · 1 stop

Phase 3 connects downtown Tampa to the University of South Florida. the third-largest university in Florida by enrollment. via a 12.5-mile alignment with 14 stations. A 1.6-mile Grey Line spur serves the medical district and the VA hospital.

USF generates 47,000 daily inbound trips. The University has signed a memorandum of understanding committing to a $40M station-area cost share and a transit-pass program for all enrolled students, faculty, and staff.

The corridor uses the Nebraska Avenue / Florida Avenue alignment, with median running through Tampa Heights and at-grade signalized operation north of Bearss Avenue.
