Revenue bonds can accelerate construction.

The Trust Fund's dedicated revenue stream is bondable under § 212.055, Fla. Stat. Issuing 30-year tax revenue bonds in Year 1 lets the County deliver Phase 1 construction on a compressed timeline. front-loading capital instead of waiting decades to accumulate it. Debt service is paid out of surtax proceeds; service is not used as collateral against other county obligations.

§ 03CPerpetual Funding Model

A Transportation Endowment Fund.

Modeled after the innovative endowment originally established for the TECO Line Streetcar, the Tampa Transit Master Plan proposes a dedicated Transportation Endowment Fund as a long-term tool for keeping fares free and supplementing system operating costs.

A one-time capital contribution is placed into a professionally managed endowment, invested in perpetuity. Only the annual distributions. typically four to five percent of the fund's value. are deployed toward operations and maintenance. The principal is never spent.

By charter, distributions are restricted exclusively to system operating costs and fare subsidies. ensuring that what is invested in Tampa's transit stays in Tampa's transit.
