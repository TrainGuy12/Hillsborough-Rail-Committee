CSX Spur & Streetcar

CSX Spur Line + Streetcar Extension. CSX Spur 11.6 mi · 16 stops · Streetcar Ext 5.0 mi · 18 stops. Projected to carry 14.9M / year (Yr 5).

CSX Spur 11.6 mi · 16 stops · Streetcar Ext 5.0 mi · 18 stops

Phase 1 reuses the disused CSX freight spur as a 11.6-mile rapid-transit alignment with 16 stations, anchoring at Ybor City and threading north through the urban core. It pairs with an 18-stop, ~5-mile streetcar extension that builds out from the existing TECO Streetcar to densify service in central Tampa.

This phase delivers the most riders per dollar of any in the program. the CSX corridor already has graded, single-tracked right-of-way that needs only re-railing, signaling, and electrification. The streetcar extension uses existing public street ROW.

Phase 1 is the political and operational keystone. Its ridership numbers will determine federal Capital Investment Grant scoring for every subsequent phase.
