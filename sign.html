Sign the Petition.

The amendment reaches the November 2026 ballot when 150,000 verified signatures are filed by February 1, 2027. Florida law requires wet-ink signatures from registered Hillsborough County voters.

Three minutes, a stamp, and your name on the ballot.

The Florida Constitution requires citizen-led ballot amendments to gather signatures by hand. We can't sign for you online. but we can make every other step take less than five minutes total.

1
Submit your details below

Name, address, and email. We confirm your registration with the Supervisor of Elections.

2
Receive a petition form by mail

Pre-printed, pre-addressed, with a postage-paid return envelope. Arrives in 3-5 business days.

3
Sign, witness, mail back

Sign in the presence of any adult witness. Drop in any USPS box. We do the rest.
