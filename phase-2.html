Airport & Cypress

Airport + Cypress Line. 6.6 mi combined · 10 stops. Projected to carry 7.4M / year (Yr 5).

6.6 mi combined · 10 stops

Phase 2 connects Tampa International Airport to the Phase 1 spine via two integrated branches: the Airport Line and the Cypress Line. Together they bring 24M annual TPA passengers into the rapid-transit network for the first time.

The corridor parallels existing roadway and rail alignments in Westshore, with at-grade running and a short elevated section approaching the airport's people-mover terminus.

Federal aviation funding under FAA AIP § 47109 is eligible for the airport-adjacent segment, reducing local match by an estimated $90M.
